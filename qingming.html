Qingming Festival:

Tomb-Sweeping Day

A traditional Chinese festival of the first day of the fifth solar term of the Chinese lunisolar calendar. Chinese Memorial Day (Ancestors Day) The festival last for 3 days, and usually takes place on the 4th or 5th day of April. The holiday is a perfect example to the celebration of the virtue of Xiao , known as the traditional value that children hold for their parents, and is meant as a child's holiday to celebrate and remember relatives and ancestors who have passed away
